User Guide

Complete guide to using all features of Lens Rx App

Dashboard Navigation

The dashboard is your central hub for quick access to all features:

  • Add Prescription: Create new patient prescriptions
  • View Prescriptions: Access your prescription history
  • Reports: Generate business analytics and reports
  • Profile: Manage your clinic information

Creating Prescriptions

Follow these steps for accurate prescription entry:

  • Fill in patient personal details (name, age, gender)
  • Enter prescription details for both eyes (SPH, CYL, AXIS)
  • Select vision type (Distance, Reading, Bifocal)
  • Add lens specifications and special instructions
  • Include payment information and amount

Managing Prescriptions

Efficiently manage your prescription database:

  • Use the search bar to find specific prescriptions
  • Filter by date range, patient name, or prescription type
  • Edit existing prescriptions with one click
  • Delete outdated or incorrect entries
  • Export data for backup or analysis

Printing & Sharing

Multiple options for prescription output:

  • Print directly to thermal printers (58mm width)
  • Save as PDF for digital records or email
  • Share via WhatsApp with patients instantly
  • Customize print layout and information displayed
