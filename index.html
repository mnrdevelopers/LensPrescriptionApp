How It Works

Get started in just a few simple steps

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email address and create a secure password. Your data will be automatically synced across all devices.

2

Setup Your Clinic Profile

Enter your clinic details including name, address, contact information, and optometrist details. This information will appear on all prescriptions.

3

Add Patient Prescriptions

Use our comprehensive form to enter patient details, prescription data for both eyes, vision type, lens specifications, and payment information.

4

Sell Products & Manage Inventory

Add eyewear products to your store, manage inventory levels, and process customer orders with integrated payment processing.

5

Print & Share

Generate professional prescriptions that can be printed, saved as PDF, or shared via WhatsApp with your patients instantly.

User Guide

Complete guide to using all features of Lens Prescription App

Dashboard Navigation

The dashboard is your central hub for quick access to all features:

  • Add Prescription: Create new patient prescriptions
  • View Prescriptions: Access your prescription history
  • Products Store: Browse and manage eyewear products
  • Inventory: Manage product stock and categories (Admin)
  • Reports: Generate business analytics and reports
  • Profile: Manage your clinic information

Creating Prescriptions

Follow these steps for accurate prescription entry:

  • Enter complete patient demographics
  • Fill both DIST and ADD sections for each eye
  • Specify SPH, CYL, AXIS, and V/A values accurately
  • Select appropriate vision, lens, and frame types
  • Record payment details and amount

E-Commerce Features

Sell eyewear products with our integrated store:

  • Product Catalog: Browse available eyewear products
  • Shopping Cart: Add products and manage quantities
  • Secure Checkout: Razorpay integrated payments
  • Order Management: Track customer orders
  • Inventory Control: Real-time stock management

Printing & Export

Multiple ways to share prescriptions:

  • Thermal Print: Optimized for 58mm receipt printers
  • PDF Export: Professional digital copies
  • WhatsApp Share: Instant sharing with patients
  • Preview: Review before finalizing

Managing Records

Efficiently manage your prescription database:

  • Search by patient name or mobile number
  • Filter by date range and prescription types
  • View grouped by Today, Yesterday, and Older
  • Delete outdated records securely

Reports & Analytics

Track your practice performance:

  • Daily, weekly, and monthly reports
  • Custom date range analysis
  • Prescription count and revenue tracking
  • Sales reports for product sales
  • Exportable report data
