User Guide

Complete guide to using all features of Lens Prescription App

Dashboard Navigation

The dashboard is your central hub for quick access to all features:

  • Add Prescription: Create new patient prescriptions
  • View Prescriptions: Access your prescription history
  • Reports: Generate business analytics and reports
  • Profile: Manage your clinic information

Creating Prescriptions

Follow these steps for accurate prescription entry:

  • Enter complete patient demographics
  • Fill both DIST and ADD sections for each eye
  • Specify SPH, CYL, AXIS, and V/A values accurately
  • Select appropriate vision, lens, and frame types
  • Record payment details and amount

Printing & Export

Multiple ways to share prescriptions:

  • Thermal Print: Optimized for 58mm receipt printers
  • PDF Export: Professional digital copies
  • WhatsApp Share: Instant sharing with patients
  • Preview: Review before finalizing

Managing Records

Efficiently manage your prescription database:

  • Search by patient name or mobile number
  • Filter by date range and prescription types
  • View grouped by Today, Yesterday, and Older
  • Delete outdated records securely

Reports & Analytics

Track your practice performance:

  • Daily, weekly, and monthly reports
  • Custom date range analysis
  • Prescription count and revenue tracking
  • Exportable report data
